Weekly Learning Routine

  • Day 1-2: Focus on the primary course of the month (1-2 hours/day)
  • Day 3-4: Practice skills and work on small projects (1-2 hours/day)
  • Day 5: Supplementary learning (related articles, videos, or additional courses) (1-2 hours)
  • Day 6: Review and practice (1-2 hours)
  • Day 7: Rest and reflect on progress

Tips for Success

  • Stay Consistent: Allocate regular time slots for learning and practice.
  • Apply Learning: Work on projects to apply what you've learned.
  • Seek Feedback: Share your work with peers or mentors for constructive criticism.
  • Build a Portfolio: Document your progress and showcase your best work.
  • Network: Join online communities and forums related to your fields of study.
